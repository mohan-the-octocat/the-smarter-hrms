# 14.4 Pre-Approval Requirements

Pre-Approval Requirements Business courtesies of more than US$100 per person in value require written pre-approval because they may create an appearance of impropriety.
- Under US$100: No pre-approval is required.
- Over US 250: Requires written pre-approval from the employee's manager. 100 𝑢𝑝𝑡𝑜 𝑈𝑆
- Over US 500: Requires written pre-approval from the employee's director 250 𝑢𝑝𝑡𝑜 𝑈𝑆(or country manager if there is no director in-country).
- Over US$500: Requires written pre-approval from the employee's VP.
